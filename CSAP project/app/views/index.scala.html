Welcome to CSAP



CSAP collects all the Computer Science graduated students around the country and have access to them.
Keeping a clear line of communication between graduated students and the faculty of computer science is a very big challenge to be solved and it can be taken care of using several methods.CSAP method is a solution that can be offered and keep the communication line clear between both of them closely.
By this application Computer Science faculty can put an announcement of their latest updates,scholarships, and job offers to notify all the students about new opportunities so students can apply to their favorite section .
